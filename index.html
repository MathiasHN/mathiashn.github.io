J.Views—401 Days

During a period of 401 days, J.Views shared the making of his next album step-by-step. The process, dubbed The DNA Project, gave fans access to every place, person and sound involved in J.Views’ creative process. The album 401 Days includes visual recaps of selected days of the DNA Project. With map coordinates from the varying locations in which the songs were created and select images from the J.Views’ 401-day journey.

Biography

I’m a passion driven Design Director from Denmark with 10 years of industry experience. I have a holistic approach to everything I do and think it’s important not only to love the craft but also the story behind.

I'm passionate about creating purposeful brands, platforms and products. I strive to create things that can stand the test of time and at the same time provide value to both the user and the brand.

I’ve gained international experience within my field, by working with some of the most respected brands, startups and NGO’s in the world: Google, YouTube, Guggenheim, NASA, B&O, IKEA, SPACE10, Muuto, Vice, Red Bull, Microsoft, Foursquare, Roambi, ECCO, Ramlösa, Red Cross and Civil Rights Defenders. Throughout this work I’ve been honored some of the most prestigious awards within the industry.

I’m living in Copenhagen, running Norgram Studio with my co-founder Sebastian Gram.

Formerly Hello Monday, Sr. Art Director & Designer (New York & Copenhagen).
Interactive Art Director graduate from Hyper Island, Stockholm.

Skills

Art Direction & Concept Development, Digital Design, Platforms & Products, Brand strategy & Visual identities, Process design & facilitation, Workshops & Lectures.
